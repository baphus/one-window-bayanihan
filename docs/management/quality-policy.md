## Policy Statement

The One Window Bayanihan system is committed to providing efficient, secure, and accessible case management services to Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs), partner agencies, and DMW Region VII staff.

We achieve this through:

1. **Client Focus:** Prioritizing the needs of OFWs and their families in all system design decisions
2. **Continuous Improvement:** Regularly measuring, analyzing, and improving our processes and services
3. **Evidence-Based Decisions:** Using data and metrics to guide improvements
4. **Process Approach:** Managing activities as interconnected processes for consistent results
5. **Stakeholder Engagement:** Involving DMW staff, partner agencies, and users in system evolution

## Quality Objectives

1. Maintain case resolution SLA (see `quality-objectives.md`)
2. Achieve 99% system availability during business hours
3. Respond to incidents within defined severity SLAs
4. Conduct quarterly management reviews
5. Maintain 90%+ user satisfaction score

## Responsibilities

- **Management:** Provide resources, review quality metrics quarterly
- **Technical Lead:** Ensure development follows defined processes
- **All Staff:** Report quality issues and suggest improvements

## Review

This policy is reviewed annually by management and updated as needed.
