# Quality Objectives

**Document ID:** D60-10-QO  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2026-07-09  
**Standard:** ISO 9001 6.2  
**Owner:** Quality Management Representative

## Measurable Objectives

| Objective | Metric | Target | Measurement Method | Frequency |
|-----------|--------|--------|--------------------|-----------|
| Case resolution within SLA | % of cases closed within 30 days | ≥90% | Case completion timestamps | Monthly |
| System uptime | Availability during business hours (Mon–Fri, 8 AM–6 PM PH) | ≥99% | Health endpoint monitoring | Monthly |
| Referral response time | Time from referral creation to first action | ≤48 hours (median) | Referral timestamps | Monthly |
| Incident response | P1 incidents responded to within 15 min | 100% | Incident log | Quarterly |
| User satisfaction | Survey score (1-5) | ≥4.0 | Annual user survey | Annually |
| Test coverage | Code coverage | ≥80% | PHPUnit coverage report | Quarterly |

## Tracking

Objectives are tracked in the CAPA register and reviewed during quarterly management reviews.

## Updates

Objectives are reviewed and updated annually during the management review process.
